About Brunchcore

Brunchcore is the result of spending way too much of my life obsessing over music. Mostly wasting hours of my life making mixtapes which became obsessively creating playlists. I also dabble in some synthesizer playing, tape loops, dj-ing, and generally tinkering with making noise that sometimes resembles something musical. My tastes have evolved over time, but, these days, track toward downtempo music. I love a good obscure discovery, but my listening is incredibly vast.

I am a dedicated collector of vinyl records and my DJ sets are primarily on vinyl. I'm not a scratch DJ but I do mix in some effects from time to time. There are some tracks that either aren't available on vinyl or I just haven't acquired them in the format yet. In this case, I do mix in some digital tracks.

I love to share my music with others. I host a music & meditation program to give folks a chance to unplug and chill out and listen to music together. Contact me if you are interested in attending one of these sessions.

Brunchcore is an attempt to broaden the idea of what a DJ set can be. We don't need to limit ourselves to playing all-out bangers to a crowded dancefloor. There is music to fit every mood, and my mood is brunch.
